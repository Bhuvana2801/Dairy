Our Farm's Story

At Ayyappa Dairy Farm, we believe that happy cows and devoted care produce the purest milk. Founded in 2026 under the blessings of Lord Ayyappa, our farm is built on faith, hard work, and love for the land.

Our herd grazes on lush green pastures and is nurtured with the finest natural feed. Every drop of milk we produce carries the warmth of our devotion and our commitment to your family's health.
